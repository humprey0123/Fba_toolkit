What's Inside This Toolkit

Built as a BSNEd Group 6 research output from National Teachers College. All content reflects the researchers' findings and is designed to support pre-service SNEd teachers in understanding and applying Functional Behavioral Assessment.

Transparency Notice — AI-Assisted Website

This digital toolkit website was created with the assistance of Artificial Intelligence (AI). However, all content — the workshop program, case studies, video resources, ABC forms, BIP template — is based entirely on the findings and outputs of the researchers. The Invitation Letter and Informed Consent Form were crafted by the researchers themselves. This toolkit is offered to help future researchers and educators carry out and build upon this study.

About This Toolkit

FBA Workshop Toolkit for Pre-Service Special Needs Education Teachers

Developed by Group 6, BSNEd, National Teachers College (2025–2026). This toolkit serves as a practical resource for FBA workshops, including a workshop program matrix, case studies, data collection forms, a BIP template, video resources, and assessment instruments — designed to build FBA competency among pre-service SNEd teachers.
